Process Change Project Approach

A left-to-right five-step flow from design to durable adoption.

01

Make case for change

Clarify urgency, risk of inaction, and the business outcomes the new process enables.

Open AI case assistant →
02

Assess change radius

Map impacted roles, teams, systems, and handoffs to scope where support and ownership must be defined.

03

Assess change readiness

Measure sponsor alignment, manager confidence, and team capability to prioritize readiness interventions.

04

Communication and alignment

Deliver concise role-based messaging through leader forums, manager cascades, and milestone updates.

05

Enable workforce

Roll out role-based training, job aids, and reinforcement loops so new process behaviors stick.
