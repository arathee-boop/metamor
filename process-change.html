Process Change Project Approach

A visual five-step flow to move from process redesign to adoption at scale.

01

Make case for change

Clarify why the process must shift now, the risk of inaction, and the business outcomes this change unlocks.

02

Assess change radius

Map impacted roles, teams, systems, decisions, and downstream handoffs to scope where support is needed.

03

Assess change readiness

Baseline sponsor alignment, team confidence, and capability gaps so interventions are prioritized early.

04

Communication and alignment

Deliver concise role-based messaging through leader forums, manager cascades, and timely milestone updates.

05

Enable workforce

Launch role-focused training, job aids, and reinforcement loops so new process behaviors stick.
