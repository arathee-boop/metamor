Process Change Project Approach

A practical five-step flow from process design to sustained adoption.

1Make case for change

Clarify why the process must shift now, the risk of staying still, and the business outcomes this change unlocks.

2Assess change radius

Identify every team, role, process handoff, and system touchpoint impacted directly or indirectly by the new process.

3Assess change readiness

Measure readiness across leaders and frontline teams, then focus support where capability or confidence gaps are highest.

4Communication and alignment

Roll out concise role-based messaging through leadership channels, manager cascades, and milestone checkpoints.

5Enable workforce

Deploy practical enablement with training, job aids, and reinforcement rituals to sustain new process habits.
